Retail Sales Analysis

Business Analytics ‚Ä¢ SQL ‚Ä¢ PostgreSQL

This project addresses the issue of limited visibility into retail sales performance and customer purchasing patterns within raw transactional data. The analysis involves building, cleaning, and querying a retail sales database using SQL to organize sales records, remove incomplete data, and explore key dimensions such as product categories, customers, and time periods. By applying aggregation, filtering, window functions, and conditional logic, the analysis uncovers insights including category-level sales distribution, high-value transactions, top-spending customers, peak sales months, and purchasing behavior by gender and time of day, enabling clearer interpretation of sales trends and customer behavior for business decision-making.
